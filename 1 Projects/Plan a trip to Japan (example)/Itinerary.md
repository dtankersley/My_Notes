
# Japan Itinerary

**Dates:** April 3–17, 2026

| Day | Plan |
| --- | ---- |
| Day 1 | Arrive Narita, check in, explore Shinjuku |
| Day 2 | Shibuya, Harajuku, Meiji Shrine |
| Day 3 | Akihabara, teamLab Borderless |
| Day 4 | Tsukiji Outer Market, Imperial Palace gardens |

| Day | Plan |
| --- | ---- |
| Day 8 | Shinkansen to Kyoto, check in, Nishiki Market |

## Notes

- Cherry blossom forecast: usually peaks early April in Tokyo, mid-April in Kyoto
- Get a Suica card at the airport for local transit
- Activate rail pass on Day 8 (Shinkansen day) to maximize the 7-day window
