Concise summary

- [[Google]] has embedded [[Gemini (language model) | Gemini]] AI into [[Google Maps]], allowing businesses to be discovered by customers through conversational AI, and businesses that adapt to this change first will have an advantage [(00:00:10)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7pCuSxsmNiw&t=10s).
- Gemini AI interprets natural language queries, and businesses need to optimize their Google business profiles to match how people actually talk, rather than just using keywords [(00:01:30)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7pCuSxsmNiw&t=90s).
- By offering Google business profile optimization for Gemini, businesses can charge $500 to $2,000 per business for an audit and optimization, and provide a custom report showing where the business is losing customers [(00:02:59)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7pCuSxsmNiw&t=179s).
- Business owners can use free or cheap tools to optimize their profiles, including rewriting descriptions to answer questions people ask out loud, and encouraging customers to leave reviews that mention specific things [(00:04:37)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7pCuSxsmNiw&t=277s).
- Google has opened up Maps Grounding in the Gemini API, allowing users to build AI agents that access live Google Maps data and create custom workflows to analyze business information and provide competitor summaries [(00:02:27)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7pCuSxsmNiw&t=147s).
