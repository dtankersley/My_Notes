## Push and Pull Prompting Methods

- To master AI, it is essential to understand the difference between push and pull prompting, where push prompting involves telling AI how to do a task, and pull prompting involves starting with the outcome and letting AI figure out the best way to get there, allowing AI to do the heavy lifting [(00:07:22)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0Tch0N5nsRU&t=442s).
- The process of using pull prompting involves giving AI the role and context, providing an outcome-based objective, asking AI to ask questions to create the desired outcome, and then answering those questions to let AI do the work [(00:08:18)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0Tch0N5nsRU&t=498s).
- Pull prompting is similar to using GPS, where AI directs the user based on the destination provided, and it is a method that software programmers have been using, known as spec-driven development [(00:08:06)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0Tch0N5nsRU&t=486s).
- To refine the outcome, users can ask AI to ask more questions to make it more relevant, and this process can be repeated to achieve the desired result [(00:08:58)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0Tch0N5nsRU&t=538s).
