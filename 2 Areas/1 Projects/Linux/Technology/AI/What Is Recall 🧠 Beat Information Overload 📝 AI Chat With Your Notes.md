## How to Export Notes from Recall to Obsidian
- To export your notes from [[Recall]] to [[Obsidian (software) | Obsidian]], open the note you want to export.
- Click on the three dots (options menu) located in the top right corner of the note.
- Select the option **"Export to** [[Markdown]]**"** from the dropdown menu.
- This will generate a Markdown file of your note, which you can then open directly in Obsidian or import into your Obsidian vault.
- Exporting to Markdown allows you to have offline access to your notes and integrate them seamlessly with other knowledge bases like Obsidian for long-term storage and organization.
- This workflow enables you to do your research and note-taking in Recall and then transfer a "snapshot" of your knowledge to Obsidian for further use and development [(00:23:33)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0hUb5Lv7BsY&t=1413s).
This process ensures you maintain control over your information and can continue building your knowledge base across both platforms.
