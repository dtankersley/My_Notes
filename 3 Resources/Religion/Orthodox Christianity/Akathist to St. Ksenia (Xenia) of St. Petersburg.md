Concise summary

- St. Ksenia of [[Saint Petersburg | St. Petersburg]] is honored for her holy memory and her ability to intercede for souls.
- She lived a life of voluntary poverty, distributing her wealth to the poor and enduring hardships such as hunger and cold.
- Through her foolishness for the sake of [[Jesus | Christ]], she acquired gifts such as clairvoyance and the ability to prophesize, which she used to help those in need and bring relief to the suffering.
- St. Ksenia of St. Petersburg is revered for her endurance of illness, poverty, hunger, and thirst, and for her devotion to [[God in Christianity | God]].
- She is praised for her foolishness, which reproved the madness of the world, and for her weakness, which put the mighty and wise to shame.
- St. Ksenia is considered a protector of the Orthodox family, a denouncer of drunkenness, and a helper in times of trouble, and is asked to intercede for the salvation of souls.
- St. Ksenia of [[Saint Petersburg | St. Petersburg]] is honored for her unique way of life and her ability to heal through Christ's strength.
- She gave up her wealth to live in poverty, rivaling the Angels, and was granted glory and honor by God.
- St. Ksenia is considered wise and holy by the faithful, and is now dwelling in the Father's house after her earthly wandering.
